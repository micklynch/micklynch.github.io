About Me

Hey, I'm Michael — a product manager who's spent close to two decades building and launching tech that actually makes a difference. Mostly, I focus on AI in healthcare — getting smart solutions into the hands of patients (millions of them, in fact) and clinicial teams.

I’ve led transformations, started a company, and still love the nerdy side of things enough to teach and contribute to open-source projects. My early graduate work was all about building data sets to train computers to do human-like tasks — a foundation that still shapes how I think about AI today.

If you’re into AI, healthcare, or just a good chat about where the two meet, you can grab some time with me here.
